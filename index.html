Live pest or contamination found — act now
1停止作业，就地封闭集装箱 Stop work, close container
2隔离并固定现场，拍照留证 Isolate scene, photograph
3立即致电 0800 80 99 66 Call the Pest & Disease Hotline
4按MPI/BACC指示行动，未获授权不得放行货物 Act on MPI direction only
